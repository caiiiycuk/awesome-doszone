DOS Zone
========
Dos Zone Project is a team of enthusiasts that advocates for the preservation of cultural gaming heritage by combining it with modern technology and strives to make games accessible.

Within the project, we endeavor to comply with global and, first and foremost, Russian legislation, while also making every effort to operate within the legal framework. We are against any form of piracy, illegal distribution of content, and infringement of anyone's rights.

We have no monetization; we do not receive any benefit or profit. There is no advertising or other means of extracting material gain. We use our own unique developments, abandonware, and open-source software with open licenses, and in accordance with the terms of various GPL licenses, we consciously publish the code of our projects.

We are always ready to promptly remove any disputed content upon the first official request and are open to official cooperation regarding the hosting and porting of games for the web.

Every member of our team is ready to stand behind each of the above statements and adheres to the established ideology.

Sincerely,
[The DosZone Team](https://dos.zone/team/)
